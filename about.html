Who We Are

Asas Al Waha Technical Services LLC provides integrated technical solutions for buildings and facilities across Dubai. We support owners, property managers, and developers with dependable service, clear communication, and high-quality workmanship.

Our teams handle day-to-day maintenance, corrective works, and project-based upgrades for residential, commercial, and hospitality assets.

  • Skilled technicians with trade-specific experience.
  • Use of appropriate materials and tools for long-term performance.
